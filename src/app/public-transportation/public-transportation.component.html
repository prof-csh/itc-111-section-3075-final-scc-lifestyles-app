Public Transportation

Bus

The area that King County covers ranges from its most northern point of Shoreline all the way south to Kent. The King County Metro provides over 200 bus routes that covers the entirety of King County. Besides the regular buses, King County Metro also provides other services such as the RapidRide and the Night Owl. The hours that the King County Metro operates vary depending on the bus route but typically runs from 5:00 AM to 1:00 AM on weekdays and 6:00 AM to 1:00 AM on weekends. The Night Owl is the exception where there are a few bus routes that will continue to run from midnight to 5:00 AM.

Train

Seattle has a total of 4 major forms of train transportation. Link Light Rail - The Light rail runs from the University of Washington station to the Angle Lake station (heading southbound) . The train has a total of 13 stops and runs every 6, 10, and 15 minutes most days. The train operates from Monday through Saturday starting at 5 am to 1 am but on Sunday and holidays it runs from 6 am to midnight. Sounder Train - The Sounder train travels from Everett and Seattle (southbound) and Lakewood and Seattle (northbound). The Sounder has a total of 12 stops and runs mornings and afternoons on weekdays. Seattle Streetcar - The Seattle streetcar is a modern low-floor streetcar that is owned by the Seattle Department of Transportation in Seattle, Washington. The Seattle streetcar arrives every 10 to 15 minutes most of the day but not during the late nights. The streetcar has total of 10 stops that stretches up to 2.5 miles. Seattle Central Monorail - The Seattle Central Monorail has a total of 2 stations and runs every 10 minutes from Westlake Center Station to the Seattle Central Station. The monorail runs daily.

Airport

The main Airport in the Seattle metropolitan area is the Sea-Tac International Airport located at 17801 International Blvd, Seattle, WA 98158. The number to the Sea-Tac International Airport is (206) 787-5388. The Sea-Tac International Airport is ranked number 9 in the United States for passenger activity among all U.S. airports.

Taxi

The most well known taxi services in Seattle are the Seattle Yellow Cab and the Seattle Airport Taxi. Both the Seattle Yellow Cab and Seattle Airport Taxi serve Seattle not only with the basic taxi services but they also provide package and luggage service.
